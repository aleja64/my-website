Our Story

About SV Madras Kadai

A Malaysia-based boutique inspired by the timeless charm of Chennai, bringing authentic Indian sarees and elegant jewelry closer to you.

Rooted in Tradition, Styled for Today

SV Madras Kadai was born from a simple idea – to make authentic South Indian sarees and jewelry easily accessible within Malaysia, without compromising on quality, craftsmanship or elegance.

We carefully source sarees such as Silk, Banarasi, Kanchipuram, Organza, Designer, Bridal and Partywear, along with curated necklaces, earrings, bangles, bridal sets, antique and oxidised jewelry.

Every piece is selected with an eye for detail – fabric feel, colour, weaving style and how it will look in real Malaysian celebrations, from temple visits to grand weddings.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver quality Indian fashion in Malaysia with a seamless and personal experience:

  • Curated collections that feel truly South Indian.
  • Honest pricing and clear communication on fabric, work and care.
  • Direct WhatsApp support for photos, videos and styling suggestions.
  • Convenient delivery options across Malaysia for your big days.
